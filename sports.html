Nissan SuperGirl Surf Pro Oceanside Pier: Aug 14 - 16, various times

The Nissan Supergirl Surf Pro is the action sports industry’s largest all-female competition and lifestyle series, featuring more than 150 female pros in surfing, skateboarding and snowboarding. Best of all, admission to the event is free! The Nissan Supergirl Pro plays a critical role for surfers looking to solidify their position on the ASP Women’s World Championship Tour (WCT). In addition to presenting the best female surfing on the planet, the event supports an eco-awareness platform that features more than 10 sustainability organizations as well as a surf industry village, live music, seminars, yoga, fashion, and autographs from the pros.
